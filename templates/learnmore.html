Leaf Disease Detection

Welcome to the Leaf Disease Detection Project, where AI helps protect crops from diseases, ensuring the sustainability of our food production.

Model

We meticulously chose a ResNet-18 model, refining it with a tailor-made dataset, yielding a remarkable 98% accuracy, showcasing our commitment to excellence in AI.

Supplement

We have implemented a specialized route that consolidates an extensive selection of disease-related supplements. By leveraging our disease recognition system, users can effortlessly access precise product links once they identify the specific leaf ailment.

ChatBot

We have seamlessly integrated a sophisticated chatbot specialized in providing responses to inquiries related to diseases. This chatbot has undergone meticulous fine-tuning on a comprehensive dataset, ensuring its effectiveness and accuracy in delivering disease-related information.
